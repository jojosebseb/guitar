Tonewoods
Each tonewood are carefully inspected before the building process ensuring that our finished guitars stay in your hands longer than inside the case.
Sitka Spruce
The sitka spruce produces a bell like trebles. Has a pale appearance with a tint of yellow. Making it a good soundboard, combined with rosewood to compliment the low ends.
Red Cedar
With a slightly softer texture than spruce, red cedar produces a warm and mellow tone. Very suitable for jazz or fingerpickers but also has an interesting strumming tone.
Mahogany
Mahogany has alot of interesting features, from its balanced tone and varying appereance. It could be used as a soundboard or a bodywood as well.
Rosewood
Regarded by many as the holy grail of bodywoods, the rosewood has a dark-brownish feel that projects a strong and deep bass.
Maple
Has a very hard stiffness, maple greatly increases clarity for both high and low ends. Has a creamy white color, can be used as a soundboard or commonly a bodywood.
